Apply Your Talents at Bernard's Today

Sign up for one of our many occupations

Cashier $14.50/hr

A starter job that requires the employee to take orders and pass them to the kitchen, and handle the cash register to make sure the proper amount of money is there before and after their shift.


Janitor $14.50/hr

A starter job that requires the employee to clean the floors and tables after the customers have finished so that more customers may take their place. The janitor is also required to clean the bathrooms at least once during their shift.


Cook $16.50

An intermediate job that requires the employee to prepare the food that customers will enjoy. They are to do as the head chef or manager instructs, and most likely will be the one to read the orders off.


Fry Cook $17.00/hr

An intermediate job that requires the employee to prepare the fried foods specifically. This is ideal so that our fry cooks can focus on fried foods, while normal cooks can focus on other cooked dishes for maximun quality.


Head Chef $20.00/hr

An advanced job that requires the employee to cook more advanced dishes and also keep an eye on the other cooks. The head chef can make commands for the other cooks, however the building manager still has priority.


Building Manager $27.50/hr

An advanced job that requires the employee to make sure the establishment is running up to code, and all employees are on task. They are required to handle important buissness calls from higher up in the company and schedule meetings.
